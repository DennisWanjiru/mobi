How it works

You request

At M-tracker you are expected to login first in order to successfully make a new request. A request can be of types; maintenace or repair. We also need your location :)

We approve it

After you've successfully made a request. It is received by our happy team and analyzed. Once its analysed it is either aprroved or rejected with a reason of course.

You pay

If your request has been successfully been approved, you are notified and you are also ecpected to pay for the services prior receiving them.

We serve you

Here comes the fun part! Our team is sent to the location you provided in the first step and professionally offers the services you requested.

M-tracker

M-tracker helps you to make requests for maintenance/repair services. We will receive the request and either approve or reject it. If the request is approved the you are notified and you get the appropriate services as you requested.
